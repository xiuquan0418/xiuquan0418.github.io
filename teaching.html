Teaching Experience

My primary teaching responsibilities are in the Math department where I teach courses focused on Calculus, Linear Algebra and Statistics. I have taught fifteen different courses while at Tougaloo - fourteen for math undergraduate major and one for Jackson Heart Study Scholars. I have taught courses for our department as well as a number of Natural Science Division (e.g., NSD 221 Secret of life and CRE course development for Introduction to Biological Research etc.). A review of my student and peer evaluations will demonstrate a consistently high rating of effectiveness as well as a commitment to improvement and professional growth.
