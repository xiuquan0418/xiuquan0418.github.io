Differential Equations, MAT 316

Instructor: Xiuquan Wang

Course information

Ordinary Differential Equations. First- and second-order equations; series solutions; Laplace transform solutions; higher order equations.

Course Format

This is a lecture course (3 x 50-min lectures per week), but students will be expected to keep up with course readings, homework, and to participate actively in class. I believe that the best way to learn mathematical concepts is by actively putting them to use. The majority of the grade will therefore be based on homework assignments, quizzes, two midterm and one final.

Participation:

Participation in real-time virtual class sessions, and online on Moodle is strongly encouraged. Your questions (and answers) will help both you and your fellow students. Students are expected to attend each scheduled class and to participate fully in-class exercises and discussions. Please have your video (with whatever background you prefer to use) turned on, unless you have an unexpected difficulty or have arranged with me otherwise
