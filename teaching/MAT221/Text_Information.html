Calculus Text Information

For Fall 2020: MAT221, MAT222, MAT321 and MAT322 will use Stewart's Early Transcendentals 9th edition, published by Cengage Learning.

This book is included as an e-book in Cengage's WebAssign online homework system, which is required. In addition, an optional hard copy is available for rent from Cengage, or students can purchase at higher cost a Cengage package from the bookstore, or direct from Cengage, that includes a looseleaf paper text in addition to the e-book.

WebAssign requirement

Every section of MAT221, MAT222, MAT321 and MAT322 will have a WebAssign component integrated with the text. WebAssign costs money, but the FIRST TWO WEEKS ARE FREE and you should test drive it during drop/add. Your WebAssign class site will become available for viewing on the day of or the day before the start of classes.

To get access to WebAssign: Simply go to the WebAssign link on the Canvas course home page and follow the instructions in the "Start Here" page. There is no course key -- you register for your section's WebAssign class through your class Canvas site.

If you have had a previous WebAssign account and if your username on your old account does not exactly match the username on the new one contact Student Support at WebAssign to sync your new account to the old one.

Purchase Options

Flexible Option: Cengage Unlimited

The most flexible option and recommended option is called Cengage Unlimited https://www.cengage.com/unlimited. We recommend what is called "Cengage Unlimited 4-month", $119.99 purchased online or at the bookstore, which gives you the following:

  • Access to the e-text and WebAssign for every semester you are taking Calculus at TC as long as we are using this text.
  • Additional access for 4 months to any textbook published by Cengage being used in any TC course you are taking. For the full list of TC courses available click here. Note that the "4 month" in the name refers to access to other textbooks, and does not limit your Calculus access.
  • The option to rent a hard copy of the textbook for the semester from Cengage for $8.00 with free shipping.
If longer access to the complete list of TC Cengage courses is something you want, you can purchase instead an Unlimited 12-month option for $179.99 or Unlimited 24-month for $239.99. They all cover Calculus as long as you need it.

One Semester Option: the Bookstore's Opt-In Program

The cheapest choice for just one semester of calculus is the one semester access (Fall 2020 price: $75) using the "opt-in" program through the TC Bookstore.

The opt-in URL is https://www.bkstr.com/tougaloostore/home and is open from approximately two weeks before classes start and closes shortly after the semester starts. If you miss the opt-in window you will have to purchase access using one of the other options.

After opting-in you will receive your materials via e-mail within a few hours, with the subject line "Digital Material For ___ Has Arrived!"". This e-mail will contain a link to the Canvas login and an access code for MindTap and the etext which you can use to register from within your Canvas course. After opting-in the charge of $75 will be posted to your TC account. Note that the opt-in one semester access includes the e-text and WebAssign, but does not include the option to rent a hard copy for $8.

If you opt-in and then realize you will need Cengage Unlimited, you can still pay the difference to upgrade within the first two weeks.
