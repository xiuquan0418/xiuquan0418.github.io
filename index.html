I am currently an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Tougaloo College and Research Investigator at MCCTR at the University of Mississippi Medical Center. I graduated with a PhD in Mathematics from Southern Illinois University at Carbondale in August 2014.

My research generally focuses on the use of mathematical modeling to study cardio-renal axis, machine learning for data interpretation, and visualization of high dimensional data sets for pattern recognition. The commonly used methodologies include: Feature selection or Extraction, Topological data analysis, Visualization of high dimensional data
